✦ Constellation

A star-field word game. Pick a variant to play — each one has a ⚙ dev panel (top-left) to tune parameters live and copy the settings.

Classic Balanced default. Word letters hidden among decoy constellations. Noise stars Adds loose distractor stars that aren't part of any letter, with fewer decoys. Hard skew Heavier rotation, skew, and scale variance — letters are harder to read.
Variants live in /variants. Engine: js/constellation.js · presets & tunables: js/config.js.
